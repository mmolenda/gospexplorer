THE PURPOSE of this tool is to visualize Gospels' structure and occurrence of individual fragments across the Gospels as well as to facilitate reading of related fragments

INTENSITY of the color is proportional to the number of occurrences of individual fragment in Gospels - darker color indicates higher number of occurrences

THE GOSPEL according to Matthew - Mt, Mark - Mk, Luke - Lk, and John - J

CLICK on selected fragment's title in order to show its content and related fragments from other Gospels. Click on the title to reset

DRAG selected Gospel vertically i order to change its position

TRANSLATIONS become from Douay-Rheims Bible + Challoner Notes; titles' translation and the structure are based on NRSVCE and Polish Millennium Bible
